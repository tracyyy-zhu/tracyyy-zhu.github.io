Tracy Zhu

MS student, Statistics Department, University of Chicago

Hello! I am Tracy Zhu. I’m currently a research assistant working with Prof. Chen Wei at Rice University working on Studying Spatially Aware Visual Features in VLAs. My research interests lie broadly in generative models, multimodal learning, and representation learning.

I recently graduated from M.S. in Statistics from University of Chicago, where I was advised by [Prof. Anand Bhattad] while he was at TTIC. I received my B.A. in Data Science and Mathematics from NYU, where I was fortunate to work with Prof. Tim G.J. Rudner at University of Toronto and Prof. Marco Morucci at Michigan State University on uncertainty quantification.

I am applying for a PhD in CS/ML starting in Fall 2026. Please feel free to reach out if our research interests align!
